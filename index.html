How It Works

6 scoring periods run through the MLB season: Mar/Apr, May, Jun, Jul, Aug, and Sep.

Each month, the top 10 home run hitters from each team's 13-player roster count toward that team's score. Scoring resets every month.

Your season total is the sum of all 6 monthly scores. Click any team to see their full roster breakdown.
